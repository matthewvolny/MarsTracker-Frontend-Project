Hello there! Did you ever wonder what it might be like to visit another planet? Well then you came to the right place. I am the mars rover 'Curiosity', though you can call me 'rovey' for short. Learn about my adventures on our neighbor planet, Mars by clicking the links below.
  • 2011
    location
    After traveling more than 352 million miles (566 million km) through space, Curiosity landed safely inside Gale Crater on Aug. 5, 2012 (Pacific Time). The rover settled down within driving distance of its ultimate destination: Mount Sharp, which is known more formally as Aeolis Mons.
